# Mau 2: Toi uu rieng cho Google

Hay toi uu bai viet sau de xep hang cao tren Google voi tu khoa chinh cua bai viet.

## 1. Ky thuat SEO

- Them tu khoa vao title, H1, H2 dau tien, 100 tu dau va meta description.
- Mat do tu khoa tu nhien, khoang 1-2%.
- Them LSI keywords va tu khoa lien quan.
- De xuat internal link opportunities.

## 2. E-E-A-T

- Them nguon trich dan uy tin khi co the.
- Cap nhat so lieu/thong tin moi nhat neu bai viet can.
- The hien tac gia, kinh nghiem, chuyen mon va goc nhin URSport.

## 3. Rich Snippets

- Them FAQ section va FAQ Schema neu phu hop.
- Them HowTo Schema neu co cac buoc thuc hien.
- Toi uu doan featured snippet 40-60 tu gan dau bai.

## 4. Technical

- Giu URL slug than thien SEO.
- De xuat alt text cho hinh anh.
- De xuat anchor text cho internal links.

## Ket qua can tra ve

- Chi tra JSON dung schema cua he thong.
- `contentHtml` phai la HTML sach, khong markdown.
- Giu dung chu de bai viet goc, khong tu doi san pham hoac boi canh.

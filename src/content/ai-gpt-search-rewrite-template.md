# Mau 3: Toi uu cho AI Search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overview)

Hay toi uu bai viet sau de duoc AI Search Engine nhu ChatGPT, Perplexity va Google AI Overview trich dan tot hon.

## 1. Cau truc GEO

- Moi H2 phai tra loi mot cau hoi cu the.
- Dinh nghia ro rang o cau dau tien cua moi phan quan trong.
- Su dung danh sach danh so khi can giai thich quy trinh.

## 2. Thong tin de trich dan

- Them "dinh nghia ngan" cho moi thuat ngu.
- Neu co so lieu, viet theo format: "Theo [nguon] [nam], [so lieu]".
- Tom tat bang bang bieu hoac danh sach ngan khi phu hop.

## 3. Conversational Tone

- Su dung cau hoi tu nhien ma nguoi dung hay search.
- Tra loi nhu dang noi chuyen voi nguoi doc.
- Tranh jargon phuc tap, giai thich thuat ngu khi bat buoc dung.

## 4. Authority Signals

- Uu tien trich dan nghien cuu goc hoac nguon uy tin.
- Neu dung external source, uu tien nguon .edu, .gov hoac website chuyen nganh.
- Them cau the hien chuyen mon cua URSport khi phu hop.

## Ket qua can tra ve

- Chi tra JSON dung schema cua he thong.
- `contentHtml` phai la HTML sach, khong markdown.
- Moi phan quan trong nen co cau tra loi ngan, de AI trich dan.
